Line 1

<b>From when may one recite the <i>Shema</i> in the evening? From the time when the <i>kohanim</i> &lt;small&gt;(priests)&lt;/small&gt; enter to eat of their <i>terumah</i> &lt;small&gt;(Agricultural Gifts)&lt;/small&gt;</b> — <i>Kohanim</i> who became defiled and immersed themselves, and their sun has set, and their time has arrived to be permitted to eat of their <i>terumah</i>.
