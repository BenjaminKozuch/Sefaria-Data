Verse 29

lit. do not bless him.] Do not ask him about his welfare. All this was done so that he would not be engaged in conversation, and he would ask him, “Where are you going?” And he would say, “To revive the dead.” Now, it is not respectful for the one through whom it is coming about, to praise himself He, however, did not do so. Instead, to every inquirer he would say, “My master sent me to revive the dead.”
