Perek 1

First palace of seven palaces and which is the hall of Adam Kadmon, and in this palace are seven gates:
The first gate:
Explanation of the round [lights] and direct [lights] including five branches
Regarding the ultimate intent of the creation of the worlds which will now be clarified in two inquiries which the Kabbalists dealt with. The first inquiry is that which the first- and the last ones inquired ..
