Rav Chanan bar Rav says: 
All are aware for what purpose a bride goes into the chuppah, but anyone who fouls one's mouth and says something disgraceful, even if that person had a divine decree of seventy years of goodness sealed and granted, that would be reversed and that person would be sentenced to seventy bad years.
