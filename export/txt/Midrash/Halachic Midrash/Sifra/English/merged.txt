Braita d'Rabbi YishmaelR. Yishmael says: ...
The Torah is expounded by thirteen hermeneutical principles:



.1)	kal vachomer (a fortiori): ...
(Bamidbar 12:14): "And the L rd said to Moses: Now if her (Miriam's) father had spat in her face, would she not be in shame for seven days!" — Kal vachomer, if the Shechinah does so, it should be fourteen days! (see Tosfoth, Bava Kamma 25a). But it suffices that a kal vachomer deduction parallel what it is deduced from; therefore, (Ibid.): "Let her be sequestered seven days outside the camp, and then let her be gathered in."
